Barrier-supporting creams help maintain hydration and comfort while protecting the skin from dryness.

Night treatments remain popular because they allow active ingredients to work while the skin naturally renews itself during sleep.

SPF products continue to be one of the most recommended skincare categories for maintaining healthy-looking skin.

7. Eye Creams

Eye creams are often used to improve the appearance of tired-looking under-eye areas and provide additional hydration.

8. Exfoliating Treatments

Modern exfoliants help improve skin texture while being gentler than traditional formulas.
