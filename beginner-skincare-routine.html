Beginner Skincare Routine

1. Start With a Gentle Cleanser

A gentle cleanser removes dirt, oil and makeup without stripping the skin. Cleansing morning and evening helps keep the skin fresh and balanced.

2. Add Hydration

Hydration is essential for every skin type. Ingredients such as hyaluronic acid help attract moisture and support healthy-looking skin.

3. Use a Moisturizer Daily

A moisturizer helps maintain the skin barrier and prevents dryness. Consistent moisturizing is one of the foundations of skincare.

4. Never Skip SPF

Daily sunscreen helps protect the skin from UV exposure and supports long-term skin health.

5. Keep Your Routine Simple

Many beginners see better results with a simple routine rather than using too many products at once.

6. Be Consistent

Consistency is often more important than buying expensive products. Regular skincare habits can make a noticeable difference over time.

Final Thoughts

A beginner skincare routine does not need to be complicated. Cleanser, moisturizer and sunscreen are enough to build a strong foundation for healthy-looking skin.
